Project Manager Intern

(May 2022 - May 2023)

Ontario Power Generation is a large utility company in Ontario, Canada, ptoducing roughly half of the province's electricity. Their key mission is to deliver affordable, reliable, and sustainable energy. To achieve this, they are actively engaged in various projects, underscoring their commitment to innovation and environmental sustainability.

As a Project Manager, I've played an integral part in the Darlington Refurbishment, one of Canada's largest clean energy project. Entrusted with key responsibilities, I've managed project timelines, collaborated with various stakeholders, and contributed to strategic decision-making processes to ensure the project's success. The biggest project I was involved with was implementing a new digital maintenance management system as part of the plant refurbishment project.

Overview:

The objective of this project was to successfully implement a digital maintenance management system (DMMS) for Unit 3, aimed at optimizing basic plant operations, improving maintenance schedules, and enhancing safety measures. As the project manager, my responsibilities included thorough project planning, engaging with key stakeholders to gather their valuable input, and analyzing the impact of the project by monitoring key performance indicators (KPIs). Throughout my tenure at OPG, I diligently tracked the identified KPIs and conducted data analysis (utilizng SQL and Power BI) to evaluate the effectiveness of the DMMS implementation in Unit 3, and determine whether it should be implemented in the other units.

Result:

Alongside other tracked KPIs, such as Maintenance Response Time and Error/Bug Rate, the System Uptime trend and Stakeholder Satisfaction were prioritized for display due to privacy constraints. System Uptime aimed for a minimum of 99.5% to ensure minimal downtime. Stakeholder Satisfaction was measured through surveys to assess the DMMS's benefits across key stakeholder groups. The dashboard provided compelling evidence of the DMMS's continuous improvement in plant efficiency. Based on this data, a recommendation was made to management to implement the DMMS in subsequent units, highlighting its proven effectiveness.

Personal Project

Mobile Transportation App MVP

Transport Plus is a comprehensive transportation app specifically designed to cater to the needs of individuals with disabilities. Design underway, stay tuned!

Problem Statement

In Canada, a significant portion of the population faces mobility challenges due to disabilities or age-related conditions. These individuals often encounter difficulties when navigating public transportation systems, which can limit their independence and quality of life. The challenges include inaccessible routes, lack of real-time information about the accessibility of different transportation options, and difficulty planning trips that accommodate their specific needs. Furthermore, the information about accessible transportation options is often scattered across different platforms, making it difficult for these individuals to find and utilize the resources they need.

Proposed Solution

Transport+ is a comprehensive transportation app specifically designed to cater to the needs of individuals with disabilities and the elderly. The app aims to provide a seamless and personalized transportation experience for users, enabling them to navigate their surroundings with ease and confidence. Key features of the app include:

  • Personalized route planning
  • Accessibility icons and filters
  • Public transportation accessibility
  • User profiles
  • Community collaboration

Transport+ addresses a significant gap in the market by providing a transportation solution tailored to the needs of individuals with disabilities and the elderly. By improving the accessibility of transportation, the app can enhance the independence and quality of life for these individuals. Furthermore, by providing real-time updates and community-driven information, Transport+ can help users navigate their surroundings more confidently and efficiently. The app also has the potential to foster a sense of community among users, as they share updates and support each other in navigating their transportation challenges. Overall, Transport+ offers a valuable tool for enhancing mobility and accessibility for individuals who often face barriers in these areas.
